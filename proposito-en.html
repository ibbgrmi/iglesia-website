Purpose

Biblical Baptist Church exists to glorify God through the implementation of the Great Commandment and the Great Commission of our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ.

Matthew 22:37-40 Jesus said to him: "You shall love the Lord your God with all your heart, with all your soul, and with all your mind..."
Matthew 28:18-20 "Go therefore and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit..."
